Insomnia Treatment and Relief

What is Insomnia?

Insomnia is a sleep disorder marked by trouble falling or staying asleep. It can cause fatigue, irritability, and difficulty concentrating. At Anchor of Hope, we provide tailored treatments like cognitive-behavioral therapy for insomnia (CBT-I) to help you overcome sleep difficulties and improve your well-being.

Signs and Symptoms of Insomnia

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Waking up frequently during the night
  • Difficulty returning to sleep after waking up at night
  • Waking up too early in the morning
  • Daytime fatigue or sleepiness
  • Difficulty concentrating or focusing on tasks
