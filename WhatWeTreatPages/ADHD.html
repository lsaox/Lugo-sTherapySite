ADHD Testing and Evaluation Services

Operating in Southlake, Anchor of Hope Counseling is dedicated to offering premier psychological services. Specializing in supporting individuals facing Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), our team at Anchor of Hope provides comprehensive ADHD testing and evaluation services. If you or your loved ones struggle with maintaining focus or managing daily tasks, we're here to provide expert assistance and guidance.

ADHD, or attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder, is a prevalent neurodevelopmental condition often identified in childhood but continuing into adulthood. Diagnosis involves a thorough evaluation of behavioral patterns, cognitive function, and medical history by experienced specialists. It's not a one-size-fits-all process, requiring a nuanced understanding of each individual's experiences. Once diagnosed, a personalized treatment plan can be developed to address their specific needs.

Understanding ADHD Subtypes

In diagnosing ADHD, healthcare professionals reference the DSM-5, a comprehensive guide ensuring consistency. It defines criteria for inattention, hyperactivity, impulsivity, symptom duration, and impact.

ADHD manifests in three primary subtypes:

  • Inattentiveness: Individuals grapple with focus and organizational challenges.
  • Hyperactivity: Restlessness and impulsive decision-making characterize this subtype.
  • Combined: Symptoms from both the inattentive and hyperactive types are present.

To create an individualized plan, you need the right specialist who can pinpoint symptoms and understand how they affect your or your loved one’s daily life.
