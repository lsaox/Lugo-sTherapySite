Depression Screening and Treatment

What is Depression?

Depression is a mental health condition marked by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and disinterest in once-enjoyed activities. At Anchor of Hope, we understand the complexities of depression and offer supportive, compassionate care to help individuals navigate through their struggles.

Signs and Symptoms of Depression

  • Suicidal Thoughts or Attempts
  • Insomnia or Oversleeping
  • Feelings of Guilt, Worthlessness, or Helplessness
  • Fatigue and Decreased Energy
  • Persistent Feelings of Sadness or Emptiness
  • Difficulty Concentrating or Remembering

At Anchor of Hope, our team diagnoses depression through a thorough evaluation, considering symptoms, medical history, and daily life impact. With an accurate diagnosis, our compassionate team can recommend personalized treatment plans, offering therapy and medication options. If you suspect depression, reaching out to Anchor of Hope is the first step toward support and relief.

Seeking Help and Finding Hope for Depression

Don’t let depression hold you back. Anchor of Hope is here to guide you toward a brighter future. Our compassionate team will support you every step of the way, helping you regain control of your life.
